Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in full-stack web applications, API development, and progressive web apps. My typical projects range from SaaS platforms to e-commerce solutions and custom business automation tools. I'm particularly experienced with React, Node.js, and Python-based projects.

What's your typical response time for inquiries?

I strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, please use the WhatsApp option or mark your email as urgent in the subject line.

Do you sign NDAs or work contracts?

Absolutely. I understand the importance of protecting your intellectual property. I'm happy to sign NDAs before project discussions and always work with clear contracts that outline project scope, deliverables, timelines, and payment terms.

What are your payment terms?

For most projects, I require a 30% deposit to begin work, with milestone payments throughout the project and the final balance upon completion. I accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, Wise, and cryptocurrency for international clients.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after project completion?

Yes, I offer several maintenance packages ranging from basic bug fixes to full-scale ongoing development and feature additions. We can discuss the best option for your needs during our initial consultation.
